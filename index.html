The Problem

Most Resumes Never Reach a Human

75% of resumes are rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems before a hiring manager ever reads a single word. In 2026, virtually every company above 50 employees uses ATS software to filter applicants automatically — and most resumes are written in a way that guarantees they get filtered out.

The job market this year is the most competitive in a decade. AI displacement, tech layoffs, and economic uncertainty mean the average job posting now receives 250 applications. The candidates who land interviews aren't necessarily the most qualified — they're the ones whose resumes are written to survive the filter.

MambaHire writes resumes and LinkedIn profiles the way hiring systems are built to read them: keyword-aligned, correctly formatted, and achievement-focused. So the right people actually see your work.

FAQ

Common Questions

How does the process work?

You fill out our intake questionnaire (about 10 minutes), we write your documents and send them within 48 hours. You review, request any revisions, and you're done.

What's an ATS and why does it matter?

Applicant Tracking Systems automatically screen resumes before a human ever sees them. Most companies use them. We write specifically to pass these systems while still reading naturally to a recruiter.

Do I need to provide my current resume?

Yes. We'll also ask for 2–3 job descriptions you're targeting so we can tailor your resume to those specific roles and their exact keyword requirements.

How many revisions are included?

One revision round for the Resume and LinkedIn packages. Two rounds for the Full Bundle.

What format will I receive my documents in?

Word (.docx) and PDF. Word so you can make future edits yourself, PDF for submitting applications.

Is this worth it if I'm not actively job hunting?

A strong LinkedIn profile generates inbound recruiter interest even when you're not looking. Many clients update their LinkedIn as a proactive career move.
